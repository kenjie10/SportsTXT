Features

On SportsTXT, you'll find a great mix of features enabling sports clubs and athletes to connect directly with their fans.

Fanzone

Connect with your fans in a dedicated space where they can share their passion and engage with your team.

Video

Make your moments feel special by offering them with sneak, interviews, live routines, and even behind-the-scenes content. We support a range of video platforms, so you can easily upload videos from your phone.

Podcasts

Weekly updates, interviews, or tip of the week? Record a podcast on your phone and share it with your community.

SMS

Want to get your fans really involved with the latest updates? Send personalized SMS messages straight to your members' phones and show them that you never miss their membership.

Photo

Feature your fans, games, training sessions or events. There's no limit to how many you choose to offer. Make it more personal by adding emojis, customising colors and including text. It's a great way to document and share all the big moments.

Polls

Get real-time feedback from your fans. Ask questions and test their knowledge with fun online quizzes and voting for you.
